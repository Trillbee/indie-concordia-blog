Heide - Opera in the Park

Join us on December 7 in Heide's Sculpture Park

Heide is delighted to present Opera in the Park this summer!

Indie Concordia will take you on a melodic journey in celebration of the start of the festive season.

Relax over a picnic in the sculpture park as you listen to a classical assortment of opera and song,

with joyful favourites including Offenbach's Barcarolle and Verdi’s Brindisi.

BYO chairs, blankets, picnic and drinks.

Includes museum admission and performance

About Us

Indie Concordia are a small group of musicians brought together by their passion for classical music.

They wish to captivate you, the audience, with their joy in music, taking you on a classical music journey

to somewhere peaceful, jubilant, melodic and memorable.
